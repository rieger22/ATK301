I had always wanted to be in a band above all else, so naturally, at fifteen I joined four at once.
Since then, I have been a member of at least four bands at a time consistently, and I can never get enough music.

In the years I have been involved in music, I have written and recorded four albums and six EP's. Two EP's and few singles were self-recorded, produced, and mastered.
Included are several images from the house shows I organize and host at my house in efforts to gain more support for local artists. The first music video, "Tindergartner" was filmed at one of these house shows.

My main band now, which I mainly sing and play guitar in is called Feed the Monkeys. I write the lyrics and music, and play drums on most of the recordings and occasionally bass as well. Check out Feed the Monkeys on Instagram by clicking the icon below!
